Brief Biography[TOP]

I became a Ph.D student in the CIDSE department of Arizona State University in August, 2013. Currently I'm working in the Optimization Lab with Dr. Guoliang Xue and my senior labmates. I received my B.S. degree in 2013, from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications. My current research interest lies in Networked Systems. Specifically, I devote my efforts on network architecture, routing, protocols, algorithms, performance and QoS in various network environments such as datacenter networks and SDN. I also have interest in optimization algorithms.
